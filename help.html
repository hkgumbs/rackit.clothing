Rack It

How it works

Welcome to RackIt! The concept is pretty simple: post bundles of clothes anonymously, leave them outside your house, then let interested users come and pick them up! Any user can browse listings and Rack them. Racking a listing means that you are interested in that bundle. Once you Rack a bundle, you will be able to see the lister's address, so the more people that have Racked a bundle, the less likely it is that it is still available. NOTE: your address will be publicly visible if (and only if) you list bundles.
